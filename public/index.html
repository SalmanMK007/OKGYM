Proof of Copyright Registration

Each copyright you register is secured by the Ethereum/Polygon blockchain and gets a unique Proof of Copyright Registraion that displays:
‍
- Your work's name and image (if appropriate).

- The copyright date.
‍
- Your name.

- Your biography, photo,
contact information.

- A digital fingerprint of your work, your name, your copyright date, that is time-stamped and encrypted permanently on the blockchain. Impossible to fake yet easy to verify.

Instantly Accessible World Wide

Your Proof of Copyright Registration is instantly accessible on mobile devices or computers...

By QR code -- physically or digitally attached to your work.

By ArtisCode -- a unique number that identifies your work.

By Link -- which you can email or embed in your website.

By NFC chip -- attached to your work.
